Toto Lynx
Landing Pages


Role: Web Design, Graphic Design
Tools: Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Snagit


Landing Pages are separate websites distinct from a company's primary website, which allow a company to provide customized content targeted to specific market segments. This is the company's first impression; professional presence is key. Prospective customers are lead to these pages through certain marketing channels. The landing pages I created for Toto Lynx use clean and structured layouts to easily tailor the content generated for each prospective customer.
